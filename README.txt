== Installation ==

Starting with SegMetrics consists of just two steps: installing and setting up the plugin.
The plugin automatically syncs to the settings saved in your SegMetrics account, so don’t forget to go through the SegMetrics setup as explained in the ‘after activation’ step!

### From within WordPress

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
1. Search for 'SegMetrics'
1. Activate SegMetrics from your Plugins page.
1. Go to "after activation" below.

### Manually

1. Upload the `segmetrics` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the SegMetrics plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to "after activation" below.

### After activation

1. Find the `SegMetrics` item in your `settings` menu.
1. Follow the instructions to connect the plugin to your SegMetrics account
1. You're done!
